Hello, I'm Elissa Day.

About This Site

I created this site from scratch one day, following my win in the University of Liverpool's Code First: Girls' HTML and CSS Competition. I have continued my passion for web design, and am currently working on a variety of projects.

Another hobby of mine is writing - fiction, not code! If you click on the 'Book' section of the navigation bar, you will be taken to the first chapter of the novel I am currently working on, 'Joined Forces'.

About Me

I am the only student in the UK (and possibly the world) studying for a degree in Egyptology with Chinese. In my 1st Year at the University of Liverpool, I have been awarded the Rankin Prize for academic achievement in Egyptology.

This was achieved on top of my involvement with three societies (Archaeology, Classics and Egyptology, LSRadio, and Fencing), as well as my work as a Student Advocate for the Widening Participation and Outreach Team.

In addition, I was selected from over 800 applicants to study abroad at East China Normal University (Shanghai), as part of the British Council's Study China Programme, where I achieved a score of 93% (Distinction) in Mandarin in the examinations at the end of the three weeks.

During my 2nd Year, I will be President of the Society of Archaeology, Classics and Egyptology, as well as the presenter of two shows on the LSRadio station. I will also continue my work as a Student Advocate, alongside two new and exciting roles - as a Campus Ambassador for SEO London, and as a Student Advisory Board Member for Pearson Education.
